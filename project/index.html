The Baird family has continued to provide the finest and most dedicated funeral services to all families in the Wayland, Naples and Dansville areas for over 100 years.

Walter E. Baird, started in the funeral business in the late 1800’s in the Scottsburg and Conesus areas, later moving to the Wayland area in the early 1900’s. The busines has been family run throught the generations and expanded upon with the purchase of two additional funeral homes. The Bairds funeral homes cover Wayland, Naples and Dansville areas.
